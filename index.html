Cards is a tool designed to provide help when creating a project.
Its main purpose is to assist Web Designers, Graphic Designers and creators of all kinds.
The main functionality consists of placing the creator in the position of having to make choices within a limited set of possibilities.
The choices made will help the creator understand the direction of the project, and will provide them with a starting point on which to develop the project.

Functionalities:
  • Main: select a card for each category.
  • Random: choices are made with hole cards.
  • Custom Picks: selected cards will be shown on this page. If no cards are selected, you will be able to choose from the entire deck without limitation.
